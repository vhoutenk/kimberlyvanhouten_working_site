Setting the Scene

A Dining Room

Winter 2020 | Digital Drawing | Professor Liz Galvez

This project sets up a scene for an unconventional dining experience where the walls and floor become the table and chairs. The form comes from simple shapes of a cube and sphere. Starting with a cube, simple shapes are subtracted to create the form of the structure. The treatment of the images comes from a desire to create surrealist experiences by combining Photoshop collaging with “dream-scape” rendering styles.
