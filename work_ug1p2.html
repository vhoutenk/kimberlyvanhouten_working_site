Looking Inward

Adding a Room to House on the Stream

Fall 2021 | Architectural Design I (UG1) | Professor Ishan Pal

The addition or revision of a room propels an analytical examination of the architectural language of the precedent house. This examination led to the understanding that House on the Stream is about facilitating a connection to environment through the way the views are framed by openings extend the eyes outwards across the landscape and bring the environment into the house. My interest was about how to create a room in the house that had the opposite effect. I wanted to create a room that drew you inward and provided a barrier between you and the environment. This space could serve as a gathering space for close friends and family or be a place of individual retreat from the exposed house.
