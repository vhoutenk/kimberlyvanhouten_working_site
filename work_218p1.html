Translations

From 2D to 3D and from Solid to void

Winter 2021 | Visual Studies | Professor Anca Trandafirescu

This projects explores the translation of forms from the two-dimensional to the three-dimensional. It aims to create ambiguity between a figure and the ground and then between the volume and the void. My thesis: create the illusion of disconnected parts by cutting through the volumes with voids.
